Data Deletion Instructions

This page explains how to request deletion of your personal data from Airport Transfer Hub.

1. What data we store

We may store the following data to provide our services:

  • Account details (name, email address, phone number)
  • Booking details (pickup/drop-off, dates, flight info)
  • Payment-related metadata (transaction references)
  • Support communications

2. How to request deletion

Please contact us using the details below and include the email address associated with your account.

Email: {{ socialIcons.email.info }}

Phone: {{ socialIcons.phone.formatted }}

Address: {{ socialIcons.location.address[currentLanguage.code] }}

3. Response time

We aim to complete deletion requests within 30 days.

4. Facebook login users

If you signed in with Facebook, you can use the same process above. We rely on your email address to identify your account, so please make sure you include it in your request.
